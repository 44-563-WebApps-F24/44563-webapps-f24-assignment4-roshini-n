Roshini Naguru presents a Greek play by Sophocles.

Oedipus at Colonos by Sophocles

[_Enter THESEUS_]

THESEUS

From many voices in the former time Telling thy cruel tale of sight destroyed I have known thee, son of Laius, and to-day I know thee anew, in learning thou art here.

CREON

What seek ye more to know?

POLYNICES

Thy father?

CREON

O dismay! Ye wound me, blow on blow.

POLYNICES

Thy hand destroyed him.

CREON

Yes. Yet lacks there not herein A plea for my redress.

POLYNICES

How canst thou clear that sin?
